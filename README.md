
# 🚀 Crypto Portfolio Tracker (Windows EXE)

## 📌 Overview

Crypto Portfolio Tracker is a powerful and intuitive desktop application built for users who want full control over their digital asset management. Designed with a focus on performance, usability, and simplicity, this tool allows you to monitor your holdings in real time without relying on complex web platforms or heavy software.

The application runs as a lightweight **.exe file**, meaning you can launch it instantly without installation or additional dependencies ⚡ This makes it perfect for users who prefer fast access and a clean environment without unnecessary background processes.

Whether you are tracking a small personal portfolio or managing multiple wallets across different networks, Crypto Portfolio Tracker provides a reliable and efficient solution to stay updated and organized.

---

## ✨ Features

🔹 **Real-Time Portfolio Tracking**
Stay up to date with live price updates and instant balance calculations. The application fetches market data continuously, ensuring your portfolio value reflects current conditions at all times.

🔹 **Multi-Wallet Support**
Easily add and manage multiple wallet addresses. View all your assets in one unified dashboard without switching between platforms.

🔹 **Profit & Loss Analytics**
Gain insights into your performance with clear profit/loss indicators. Track growth over time and understand how your portfolio evolves 📈

🔹 **Clean & Minimal UI**
The interface is designed for clarity and speed. No clutter, no distractions — just essential information presented in an easy-to-read format.

🔹 **Secure Local Usage**
All operations are handled locally on your device. Your data remains private and under your control, providing an extra layer of confidence 🔐

🔹 **Portable & Lightweight**
No installation required. Simply open the **.exe file** and start using the tool immediately, making it ideal for quick access and portability.

## 🖥️ System Requirements

To ensure smooth performance, the following specifications are recommended:

* **Operating System:** Windows 10 or Windows 11
* **Memory:** Minimum 4 GB RAM (8 GB recommended for optimal performance)
* **Storage:** At least 100 MB of free disk space
* **Internet Connection:** Required for live data synchronization

The application is optimized to run efficiently even on mid-range systems without noticeable slowdowns.
